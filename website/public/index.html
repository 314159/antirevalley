Antire Valley Subdivision

The subdivision is a group of 80 homes nestled in the hills between Fenton and Eureka. It gives the peace of the country while still being within fifteen minutes of the St. Louis City limits.

The homes are not large, making for good starter houses, but most of us like it so much we have stayed far longer. Our alleys, although typically a city feature, make safe places for children to play. That also creates back entrance garages, an attractive feature. We enjoy the lower rates of Jefferson County taxes. By and large it is a friendly neighborhood, mostly lived in by owners but with some rentals.

We have our own well so no water charges. We have a three member board of trustees that lead the home owners association. There is a common ground where residents can have activities. In the fall the hills around us are bursts of beautiful colors and we are glad that we live in such a great location.
